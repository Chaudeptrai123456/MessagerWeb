It is a legend carved into memory.

From soft brushstrokes to the blaze of battle, this portfolio unfolds the journey of a lone warrior—one who walks through past, present, and future like a living epic. Inspired by the timeless spirit of Greek mythology, each illustration becomes a chapter in a visual odyssey:
Past: where memories burn beneath the crimson tree.
Present: where shadows and choices collide.
Future: where fire and blood forge an immortal monument.
